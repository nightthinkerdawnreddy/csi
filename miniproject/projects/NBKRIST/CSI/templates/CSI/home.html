ABOUT CSI

---Key features---

Knowledge Sharing

The pillars of CSI was build with the thought of constructing a platform to share knowledge! Being a CSI member one can avail the opportunities like: Opportunity to participate in the international, national, regional and chapter events of CSI such as conferences, seminars, symposia, workshops on latest technological advancements in IT.

Contribution

CSI has become a wonderful sphere for one to contribute their works and showcase their talents. You can avail the Opportunity to offer workshops/ trainings/deliver guest lectures in educational instititues in collaboration with CSI. and also Join Special Interest Group (SIG) for research, promotion, and dissemination activities for selected domains, both established and emerging.

Organized

Being closely associated with Students the Society has developed a well-established net-work of “Student Branches” all across the country. CSI has 488 student branches with more than 90,000 Student Volunteers across the nation that is monitored in an organized way

Events

As part of its endeavor to bring together and assimilate various aspects of IT, CSI conducts premier events such as a National Convention, Regional Conferences, Theme Conferences focusing on verticals etc.

ABOUT NBKRIST CSI SB

The NBKRIST-CSI Student Branch was initiated by Department of Computer Science and Engineering on 06.01.2015

What is it today? With an overwhelming response 240 student members enrolled as CSI Student Members from the department of CSE and CSI Student Branch was established on 16th April 2015. Along with this achievement NBKRIST became the prestigious Institutional member of CSI on the same date with Membership number M10069. Now we have more than 500 students as CSI members.
